City Amsterdam:

Amsterdam is a municipality in the Dutch province of North Holland. The municipality has 905,234 inhabitants,has an area of approximately 219 km² (of which a quarter is water) and is the largest city in the Netherlands. Together with 31 other municipalities, Amsterdam forms the Amsterdam Metropolitan Area. More than 2,400,000 people live in the region.

The Netherlands brims with beauty and adventure, from its pictures que windmills and wondrous fields of tulips to its hip, art-centric capital city of Amsterdam. The most populated region of the Netherlands, historic Amsterdam is renowned for its quaint streets and scenic canals.

Amsterdam Light Festival:

Every winter a new edition of Amsterdam Light Festival is organized, with artworks that are made especially for the festival. The festival also realizes special projects in collaboration with artists, partners and various stakeholders from the city.

Amsterdam Canal Cruise:

In the evening, discover the unique atmosphere of Amsterdam during this canal cruise. Thousands of lights illuminate the bridges and iconic canal houses and give the city an almost magical look.

Amsterdam Nine Streets:

The Nine Streets are – how could it be otherwise – nine streets in the Jordaan where shopping is central. There are all kinds of nice boutiques, flagship stores of big brands, eateries, cafes, pop-up stores.
